Summary

Enigine: Unreal 5.6.1
Platfrom: PS5 and PC
Game Page: Entropy.itch.io
Git Hub (REMADE): Entropy

Entropy is an award winning title where you play as a mute protagonist wandering the caverns of a forgotten world with nothing but the light of artifact.
Entropy fits in the puzzle platformer genre with some light horror elements. The Team that worked on it consisted of 5 programmers, 4 designers and 7 artists.
The development time of the game was a total of 9 weeks.

Controbution

I worked on 2 features for this game, 1 was implmented and one was not. My first feature was the platfroms that you interact with across the game.
I made them with designers in mind implementing features as they were requested.
These features incloude but are not limited to; rumble recaction, debug platfroms, easy to make paths,
see Complete breakdown for more information on all of these. I also had to handle custome behavior like collisons and how to resulve them.

My other controbution didnt get implmented into the game. The production time was quite long for this small of a game project.
I relized while making this game that making a puzzle platformer really only had so many features that us programmers have to do.
I was orignaly in charage of all platfroms but I ended up working on only on 2 of them and I would even called that only 1.5 platforms.
This did how ever leave us with time to make more intressting features without holding up the development of the game.
With my extra time I tried to make a vine genertor in unreal. I used videos of tools like this as a reference.
I only got to the point where I could generate a single spline with no branches that could, alto not perfectly, fallow a mesh.
Read more about this in Complete breakdown .

Movement In Unity (MIU) was a personal project made in school. We got 6 weeks to work on it.
I decide I wanted to learn more about unity and movement with in it. When I searched into PSQ I applied with a personal project that was about movement in unity, so I was already confinednt in my abilties but I wanted to learn more.
I find joy in games with unique and good movement so this was right down my vally.

Controbution

I used this video as a ground work as they had a way of working within unity movement that was slightly different way then the way I had previus seen. I fallowed and worked throught the video and add my spin on it. The tutorial isnt longer then an hour but I took my time with it to make sure I understood not just how but why everything was made the way it was. My favirot part of this project was the intressing way they used a shader for debuging perpeses.

Enigine: Unity
Platfrom: PC
Game Page: Nautuls.itch.io Git Hub (REMADE): Nautuls.git

Underwater light horror game where you pilot a sub and drevers ruins of a by gone age.
This was a nine week project with 3 programmers, artist 4 and 3 designers.

Controbution

I worked on 3 different aspects of the game, altho one was left finnished but unimplemented.

I made the sonar sytem that is used in the game to help the player avoid crashing into walls and other obsticals.
It was oringaly made the idea that I would get the data from the world and bascily translate all of it to a texture to avoid using a camera but do to how diffcult that was to make and how few programmers there were we went back to using a camera instead.
In the end the logic behind the sonar and all of its functnaltiy was made by me but it was optimzed by a teamate of mine knows as Kjell Hopskin, see his work here .

I also made the dialog sytem that is in the game. It has a type writer effect, plays audio and can be called from multiple different events.
It is a simple to use tool for the desginers to get the needed dialog into the game. If I had had the time I would have made the text auto translate into the voice seeing how we used a robot voice as the ship ai.

Finnaly I made the ships hazard event sytem, I was given this task late into the projects life cyckle and was told that it might not be put in before realse.
I did however finnish one of the hazards the electricity one. I used a simple 5* scripts soultion;
1. A partent script that had 2 funtion in them On and Off.
2. Childeren scripts that used the On and Off functions, overrote them, and implmented their needed custome behavior.
3. A Mangare script that on start gahtered all scripts that inharited from the partent script
4. A Call script that told the manager to tell all of the scripts it had gahtered to call their Off functions as needed
5. A Fix it script that just Called On instead of Off
